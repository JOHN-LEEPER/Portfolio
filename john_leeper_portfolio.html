Executive Profile

I am an executive leader in construction and development, with more than four decades of experience directing some of the nation’s most complex and high-value projects, where operational discipline, strategic judgment, and decisive leadership have consistently been critical to successful delivery. My work has included billion-dollar mixed-use developments, major civic institutions, transportation infrastructure, federal projects, and nationally significant public developments across San Francisco and New York City.

Over the course of my career, I have advanced from field-level construction leadership into senior executive positions including Chief Operating Officer, Senior Vice President, Director of Operations, Project Executive, and independent strategic advisor. This progression has given me a rare end-to-end perspective across construction execution, preconstruction strategy, operational leadership, financial oversight, capital planning, stakeholder management, and executive-level decision-making.

I am often engaged in complex environments where projects require stabilization, executive alignment, operational restructuring, disciplined recovery planning, and long-range strategic oversight. My work has consistently focused on protecting capital investment, restoring confidence among ownership groups, strengthening project controls, and delivering outcomes in highly regulated, high-visibility environments where both technical precision and leadership maturity are essential.
